Each chapter has rooms with a specific design theme. Color cues are the same as the "logo" of each unit.

1

Metre
(Redefining) Space

  • Portals
  • Tight spaces, large spaces
  • Non-euclidean geometries and rooms
Should be short enough, not a lot to teach except the base portal mechanism and how to move stuff around.
Square/rectangular rooms, insipired by rulers, scale/grids as decoration elements, as well as compass roses.

2

Second
(A leap in) Time

  • Velocity (not momentum)
  • Fling
  • Catapulting
  • Timers
Circular room designs, inspired by clocks and gears. Actual clocks, digital or analogic, pendulum clocks, sundials, hourglasses ought to be used as additional inspiration.

3

Kilogram, Newton, Litre
(Critical) Mass

  • Force
  • Acceleration
    • Gravity, (projected) gravity fields
  • Momentum / energy
  • Differently weighed cubes
  • Weighing scales
    • Analogic
    • Digital
Trapezoidal rooms, like weights.

4

Mole
(Insane) Amount

  • Density
  • Quantity (what of?)
    • Filling cubes with stuff?
Not a lot to explain, should be short

5

Candela, Lux, Lumen, Decibel
(Flashing) Light

  • Light
  • Lasers
    • 1 color, multiple ones for later (Hz chapter)
    • Combining lasers / attenuating them
    • Itensity range receptors (display Ã  la VU meter)
  • Light falloff (inverse-square law)
Star-shaped rooms

6

Kelvin, Celcius
(Phase transition) Temperature

  • Infrared heats up
  • Convection (hot air baloons)
  • Thermal conductivity
  • Thermal shock (break things)
  • The floor is lava
  • Melt metal / burn wood
  • Solidify water
Intoduce water as a testing and environmental element.

7

Ampere, Volt, Watt, Ohm
(Hyrdo) Electric current

  • Boiling water powers turbines
  • Hydraulic analogy
  • Explain Ohm's law
  • Tesla coils
  • Battery charging

8

Hertz
(Audiovisual) Frequency

  • Speed of light
  • Multiple light colors
  • Explain X-ray, IR, UV, microwaves, FM radio
    • Make a reference to Portal's "Transmission received" radios
  • Speed of sound
  • Music
  • Fourier transform
